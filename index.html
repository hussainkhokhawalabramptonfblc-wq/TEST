About the Event

Canada's next generation of founders starts here.

Founder's Den 2026 is a national virtual student entrepreneurship competition hosted by Brampton FBLC in collaboration with JEC & TA. Now in its second year, the competition scales from regional to national — bringing together the brightest student founders from across Canada to compete under the theme Intelligence, Redefined.

Every competing team must integrate intelligent systems — AI, automation, or data-driven technology — into a credible, original venture. The top companies compete for a $1,500 seed-capital pool and national recognition.

Mission
Shepherd visionary founders through the venture lifecycle
From Ideation to Infrastructure to Influence — we build the next generation of builders through rigorous, real-world competition.
Theme
"Intelligence, Redefined."
All ventures must meaningfully integrate AI, automation, or data-driven technology as a core element of their business model.
Scale
National reach across four provinces
Targeting Ontario, Quebec, British Columbia, and Alberta — with 500+ registrants and 60+ schools engaged across Canada.
